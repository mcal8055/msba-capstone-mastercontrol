Feature Importance — Top 15 Terms

Mean absolute contribution of each feature (and detected pairwise interaction) to the predicted log-odds of Mx conversion. Hover bars for exact values. Interaction terms shown in orange.

Key insight: priority (engagement type) dominates at ~2× the next feature. My two engineered features — account_cluster and title_cluster — both land in the top 5. Industry, territory, and account tier do not appear in the top 15, contradicting the intuition that firmographic segmentation is where to start.

Model Comparison

Held-out test-set ROC AUC across the five modeling approaches we evaluated.

Why EBM won: The Explainable Boosting Machine achieved the highest test-set AUC and provides exact per-feature decomposition for every prediction — a capability XGBoost and random forest cannot match without approximation methods like SHAP.

Shape Function Explorer

The EBM produces an independent shape function for every feature. Each category's score is added directly to the lead's predicted log-odds of conversion. Positive values push toward "Successful"; negative values pull away. Select a feature below to see how its categories move the score.

Why a low threshold wins: At an average Mx contract value of $70,000, even a small conversion rate generates revenue that dwarfs outreach costs. The economically optimal threshold is typically at the low end of the range — pursue nearly everything above a minimum confidence floor, and let the model's ranking shape the sequencing. This dashboard makes that trade-off tangible instead of abstract.
